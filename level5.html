LEVEL 5 OF 5

Make the N Disappear

Focus: Idgham bila ghunnah (the nun vanishes completely, with NO hum, and doubles into the next letter)..
⚠️ Watch out: Kids still voice or hum the nun instead of dropping it completely and doubling the next letter: say waylul-LIL, not waylun-lil, and AL-LAA, not an-laa. Watch the trap in Al-Kafirun: its abidun-MA merge actually KEEPS a hum (idgham with ghunnah), so it is NOT this no-hum rule. Mark every merge by hand, since the child cannot see the sukun or shadda in transliteration.

🤲 Ettehiyyatu (Tahiyyat) prayer

At-tahiyyaatu lillaahi wa-salawaatu wa-tayyibaat. As-salaamu 'alayka ayyuha-n-nabiyyu wa rahmatullaahi wa barakaatuh. As-salaamu 'alaynaa wa 'alaa 'ibaadillaahi-s-saaliheen. Ash-hadu an laa ilaaha illa-llaah, wa ash-hadu anna Muhammadan 'abduhu wa rasooluh.
All greetings, prayers and good things are for Allah. Peace be upon you, O Prophet, and Allah's mercy and blessings. Peace be upon us and upon the righteous servants of Allah. I bear witness that there is no god but Allah, and that Muhammad is His servant and messenger.
Tajweed color key
👃 Ghunnah
Nasal hum about 2 counts.
🫥 Ikhfa
Hide the silent n/tanwin into a soft nasal hum.
🔗 Idgham (no hum)
The n vanishes and doubles into the next letter.
🔔 Qalqalah
Light bounce on silent q t b j d.
↔️ Madd
Stretch the long vowel, 2 to 6 counts.
🌑 Silent / sun-lam
Written but not pronounced, or glided.
