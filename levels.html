Salah Essentials

5 tajweed levels · easiest to hardest

📱 Each level adds one new tajweed sound. Start at your child's level. Tap a word's reading underneath the Arabic. Turn on 🧩 Memorize to shuffle and reorder.
1
Hold the Long Notes
Focus: Madd (the long-vowel hold)
Al-Fatiha
›
2
Hum Through Your Nose
Focus: Ghunne (the 2-count nasal hum), including the cross-word tanwin merge idgham bi-ghunnah, which belongs to the ghunne family.
An-Nas, Rabbana Atina, Allahumma Salli, Allahumma Barik
›
3
Give It a Bounce
Focus: Qalqala (the springy, vowel-less bounce on the letters qaf, ta, ba, jim, dal).
Al-Kawthar, An-Nasr, Subhaneke, Al-Masad (Tabbat), Al-Ikhlas
›
4
Hide the N
Focus: Ihfa (hide the nun sakin or tanwin into a soft nasal hum that glides toward the next letter).
Al-Asr, Quraysh, Al-Fil, Al-Falaq
›
5
Make the N Disappear
Focus: Idgham bila ghunnah (the nun vanishes completely, with NO hum, and doubles into the next letter).
Al-Maun, Al-Kafirun, Ettehiyyatu (Tahiyyat)
