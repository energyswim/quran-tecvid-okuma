Hide the N

Focus: Ihfa (hide the nun sakin or tanwin into a soft nasal hum that glides toward the next letter)..
⚠️ Watch out: English and Turkish habits push a crisp, hard N; instead soften and hide it into a hum that glides to the next letter, as in al-INSAAN (Al-Asr) and min-SHARRI (Al-Falaq, four times). The transliteration gives no script cue, so mark every ihfa spot by ear, by hand. Genuine nun-ihfa count is 1 in Al-Asr (al-insan), 1 in Quraysh (min ju'in), 1 in Al-Fil (min sijjil only, the tarmihim-bi spot is a mim rule, not this one) and 4 in Al-Falaq, so the real density jump lands at the Al-Falaq capstone. Al-Falaq also carries 5 strong qalqala stops (falaq, khalaq, waqab, al-uqad, hasad), so keep those bounces clean too.
