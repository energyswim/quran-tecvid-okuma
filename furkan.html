Tajweed color key
👃 Ghunnah / Idgham with ghunnah
Nasal hum (~2 counts) from the nose. Doubled nun/mim and ghunnah idgham.
🫥 Ikhfa
Hide a silent nun / tanwin partly, humming from the nose.
🔄 Iqlab
Turn a silent nun / tanwin into a mim before the letter ba (ب).
🔗 Idgham (no ghunnah)
Merge a silent nun / tanwin into the next letter with no hum.
🔔 Qalqalah
Light bounce on silent ق ط ب ج د.
↔️ Madd (lengthening)
Stretch the vowel: natural (2), then longer obligatory ones.
🌑 Silent / sun-lam / wasl
Letters written but not pronounced or glided over.

👃 Ghunnah / Idgham with ghunnah
Nasal hum (~2 counts) from the nose. Doubled nun/mim and ghunnah idgham.
🫥 Ikhfa
Hide a silent nun / tanwin partly, humming from the nose.
🔄 Iqlab
Turn a silent nun / tanwin into a mim before the letter ba (ب).
🔗 Idgham (no ghunnah)
Merge a silent nun / tanwin into the next letter with no hum.
🔔 Qalqalah
Light bounce on silent ق ط ب ج د.
↔️ Madd (lengthening)
Stretch the vowel: natural (2), then longer obligatory ones.
🌑 Silent / sun-lam / wasl
Letters written but not pronounced or glided over.
