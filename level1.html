LEVEL 1 OF 5

Hold the Long Notes

Focus: Madd (the long-vowel hold). give short vowels one beat, hold the long alif/waw/ya an even 2 counts, and stretch the one madd lazim to a full 6 counts.
⚠️ Watch out: Kids either clip the long vowels short, or do the opposite and stretch short vowels to 2 beats. Only long alif/waw/ya get the steady 2-count, and wa lad-DAAALLEEN needs its full 6 counts. There are no nun or mim assimilations to manage here; the one consonant change, the al + sun-letter merge in ar-Rahman, ad-Din, as-Sirat and ad-Dallin, is already baked into how they read it.
